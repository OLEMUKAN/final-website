Meet Our Chancellor Rt. Rev. Dr Hannington Mutebi

In addition to his role as Assistant Bishop, he was appointed as the Chancellor of Ndejje University2. He was installed during the university’s 23rd graduation ceremony2. His duties include presiding over all ceremonial assemblies of the university and confer degrees, diplomas, certificates, titles and distinctions2. Bishop Mutebi will also perform an overall supervisory role over the university affairs during his four-year term of office2. He is eligible for reappointment as provided by the charter administrative instruments2.

Meet Our Vice-Chancellor Rev. Canon Assoc. Prof. Olivia Nassaka Banja

PhD (University of Edinburgh), MTh(University of Edinburgh), MA (Nottingham University), BD and Dip(Ed) (Makerere University). She is a Clergywoman in the Church of Uganda, where she has served for 29 years. She is Associate Professor of African Christianity. She became Vice-Chancellor Ndejje University on 1 st January 2023. She previously served as Dean School of Education Uganda Christian University, Director of Teaching and Learning at Uganda Christian University (2014 - 2021), Dean Bishop Tucker School of Divinity and Theology at Uganda Christian University (2008 – 2014).

Course Accreditation

Ndejje University addressed rumors regarding the accreditation of its Kampala, Mengo Campus courses. The university disagreed with rumors that the courses are not accredited by the National Council Of Higher Education (NCHE).
